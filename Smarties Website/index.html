Welcome to the unofficial Smarties website dedicated to bringing information about one of Canada's most beloved colourful candy! Smarties Fact #2672

Smarties were introduced in 1937 as 'Milk Chocolate Beans'. The name was later changed in 1938 when Smarties tubes were introduced.
